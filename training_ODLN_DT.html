Exercise

What should you do/not do to aid in the success of your programme?

Dave's observations

  • Do use data tools and education to inspire the data publishers (make their lives better)
  • Don't rely on technology/platforms to solve your problem
  • Don't build the coolest app/visualisation yourself!
  • Don't forget to market other peoples success for them!
  • Do get civil society involved quickly!
  • Don't think that open data allows people to break the law.
  • Do be massively opportunistic to events (flooding)
  • Don't organise your data so that only the government can find it. (Ministry of ....)
  • Don't forget the second star!
  • Do use Open Data Certificates to help build best practices and for training.
  • Don't use RDF, and only create linked data if your data is reference data
  • Don't make all data free!?!?
