Country capacity

High capacity

  • Established open data policies
  • Strong polical backing
  • Culture of open
  • Emphasis on open license
  • Government, civil and private sector involvement

Emerging and advancing

  • Established programmes
  • Innovating with data and information delivery
  • A variety of different strengths
  • Still many challenges to overcome

Capacity constrained

  • No sustained initiative
  • Limited engagement (due to access)
  • Weakness in digital data collection and management
  • Small teams with limited longevity

One-sided initiatives

  • Established initiative in government
  • Not matched by civil society capacity and freedom to engage with the data
  • Limited private sector involvement in process
  • Lacking political freedoms to enable communities to flourish
